About AnalyzeUp

AnalyzeUp is a charity assessment tool that evaluates the efficacy of charitable orgainzations based in the United States. The aim of AnalyzeUp is to help make impactful decisions in philanthropic giving because philanthropy acts as an epoxy that helps fill the chasms of need when other funding programs fail to. Philanthropy, on every level, not only safeguards a society, but ensures a society's ability to flourish.
